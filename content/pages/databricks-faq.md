**We’re thrilled to share that Neon has signed an agreement to join forces with Databricks.** If you’re currently using Neon, below are answers to common questions regarding our expectations after the acquisition closes. Read the blog posts by [Neon](https://neon.tech/blog/neon-and-databricks) and [Databricks](https://www.databricks.com/blog/databricks-neon) for more details on our vision together.

### Will my existing Neon setup break or require changes?

No. Everything will continue to work exactly as it does today.

### Do I need a Databricks account to use Neon?

No. You can continue using Neon exactly as you do today, there’s no requirement to use any other product or platform.

### Can I still use the same channels to contact Neon support?

Yes. Nothing changes, you can continue to reach us through the same support channels you use today.

### Is the Free Plan going away? 

No. The free plan stays. 

### Will Neon still prioritize developers and startups?

Yes. Our focus on developers and startups remains unchanged. We’ll keep building for fast-moving teams and supporting them as they grow. 

### Is Neon going to be shut down?

No. Neon will continue to operate without disruption with the same team and mission.

### Are any features being deprecated?

No. All existing features remain available, and we will continue to improve and expand them.

### Will I be billed by Databricks now?

No, Neon will continue to bill users directly through our existing system. If anything changes in the future, we’ll communicate it clearly. 

* * *

 If you still have questions, here’s how to reach us:

-   If you’re on the Launch, Scale, or Business plans, you can reach us at customer-success@neon.tech.
-   If you’re on the Free plan, we’ll be available [on Discord](https://discord.gg/92vNTzKDGp). 

**To all our users: thank you for being part of this journey.** This is a very proud moment for our team, and we can’t wait to keep building together.
