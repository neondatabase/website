title: Snapshot pricing in effect, manual snapshot limit increased to 100
---

## Snapshot storage is now billed at $0.09/GB-month

Snapshot storage pricing took effect on May 1, 2026. Snapshots are now billed at **$0.09/GB-month** — this applies to both manual snapshots and snapshots created by automated backup schedules.

Previously, snapshots were free during the beta period. To review your current snapshot usage or manage your snapshots, visit the [Backup & restore](/docs/guides/backup-restore) page in the Neon Console.

## Manual snapshot limit increased to 100 on paid plans

The manual snapshot limit on Launch and Scale plans has increased from **10 to 100**. The Free plan limit remains at 1. Snapshots created by automated backup schedules on paid plans don't count toward the manual limit.

See [Backup & restore](/docs/guides/backup-restore) for details.
