## Shorter advance notice for planned updates

We've lowered the advance notice period for planned updates on paid plans from 7 days to 3 days. You'll still see a notice in your project's settings (**Settings** > **Updates**) at least 3 days ahead of a planned update, and Scale plan customers will still receive an email notification, now 3 days in advance. This shorter window lets us keep your computes current with the latest patches while planning capacity more efficiently. To learn more, see [Updates](/docs/manage/updates).
