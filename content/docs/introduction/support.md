Pro plan users can submit support tickets through Neon's console or contact us at [support@neon.tech](mailto:support@neon.tech). Expect an initial response time of 2 business days, from 6am to 6pm Pacific Standard Time (UTC -8), Monday through Friday, excluding public holidays in the United States.

Free Tier users are not guaranteed a specific response time. For custom solutions, please contact [Sales](https://neon.tech/contact-sales).
