Neon integrates with any platform that supports Postgres database connections. Whether you’re building apps with no‑code tools, automating workflows, or creating dashboards, you can connect Neon to the tools you already use, no coding required.

## What you can accomplish

By connecting Neon to no-code and low-code tools, you can build powerful applications and workflows:

- **Workflow automation**: Connect platforms like Zapier or n8n to sync data across your apps, log events, and automatically trigger actions when your database updates.
- **Admin panels and internal tools**: Use tools like Directus or Strapi to instantly generate a full backend interface and CMS directly from your database.
- **Data visualization and analytics**: Connect BI tools like Metabase or Draxlr to turn your raw database records into interactive charts, graphs, and dashboards.
