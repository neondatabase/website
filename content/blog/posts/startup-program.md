## Why we’re doing this

At Neon, we believe that infrastructure should accelerate you, not slow you down. Whether you’re building an AI agent, a next-gen SaaS product, or a consumer app that just went viral — Neon gives you a database that scales with you automatically, branches in seconds, and integrates directly with your dev workflows.

And we’ve seen first-hand how early access to great infrastructure can make or break a product. That’s why we want to support founders from day one.

## What you get

Startups accepted into the program receive:

- **Up to $100,000 in Neon credits**. Use them over 12 months for production workloads, dev environments, or anything in between.
- **Access to Neon’s product and team**. Get early access to new features and priority support direct from the Neon team.
- **Visibility and partnership opportunities**. We love sharing what our customers build — and many go on to partner with us publicly through launch stories and technical deep dives.

## What makes Neon different

Neon is the best database for developers. It’s fast, scalable, and best of all, it’s 100% Postgres. Here’s what startups love most:

- **Instant branching and environments**. Ship faster with production-like staging environments that spin up instantly and clean up automatically.
- **Built for AI-native development**. Neon works seamlessly with codegen tools, agents, and AI copilots — including Vercel v0 and Replit.
- **Autoscaling, down to zero**. Pay only for what you use. Neon scales up during traffic spikes and scales to zero when things are quiet.
- **Modern DX**. A beautiful dashboard, CLI, and APIs that your dev team will actually enjoy using.

## Who should apply

We’re looking for early-stage startups with bold ideas and strong momentum. In general, companies that:

- Have raised **$1M to $5M** in venture funding
- Have launched a product in the **past 12 months**
- Are building software that needs a fast, scalable, and modern Postgres database

Funding doesn’t need to be from one of our partners — any institutional investment will be considered. Credit amounts vary based on stage, use case, and projected scale.
