In June 2022, we presented Neon as a fully managed Serverless Postgres built for the cloud. Since then, we have worked closely with the Postgres and developer communities to help address common challenges in building modern applications.

We want to continue that partnership with the community and share some exciting things we have been working on.

**This is why we are excited to announce the first-ever Neon Developer Days, which will take place from the 6th to the 8th of December, 2022.**

[Register now](https://neon.tech/developer-days-1).

![Post image](https://cdn.neonapi.io/public/images/pages/blog/neon-developer-days-1-6-8-december-2022/screenshot-2022-11-17-at-1531-1024x571-cac6fc64.jpg)

Join us virtually to learn about Neon and how you can build better with Serverless Postgres.

See you there!
